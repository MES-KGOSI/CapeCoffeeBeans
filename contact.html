Contact and Visit us

The best way to contact us is by using the form below or the chat
button at the bottom right of your screen (when we're online).

Alternatively, you can phone us at +27 87 550 9983













Support hours are:
  • Monday to Friday, 09:00 - 17:00
  • Saturday, 09:00 - 13:00

If you contact us outside of these hours, we will get back to you on
the following working day. You can leave a message for us using any
one of our support channels!

You can also place an order online and collect it in person (the option is on the cart page).

Our store is at:
187 Imam Haron Rd, Claremont 7708, Cape Town

Store opening hours are:

  • Monday to Friday, 09:00 - 17:00
  • Saturday, 09:00 - 13:00
