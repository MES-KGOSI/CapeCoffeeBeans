Aeropress coffees

This collection features coffees that particularly shine in the Aeropress, especially in inverted, long filter-style brews. Many also work well with other methods. The list highlights top picks, though the Aeropress is versatile and many other coffees can still produce great results.

French coffees

This collection showcases coffees we and our roasters find exceptional in the French press. Many also suit other brew methods. It’s not exhaustive absence here doesn’t mean a coffee won’t work just a selection of favourites.

Beans to cup coffees

This page highlights coffees suited for bean-to-cup machines, based on testing and feedback. Since these machines make espresso-style drinks with less control, slightly darker roasts work best, especially for milk-based beverages. Recommendations balance solubility, flavour, and customer preferences.

Pour-over coffees

This collection highlights coffees we enjoy as pour-overs and traditional filter brews, where paper filtration enhances their flavours. Many suit other methods too. It’s a starting point, though other unlisted coffees may also work well in filter-style brewing.

Expresso coffees

This page features coffees we and our roasters recommend for espresso, also suitable for moka pots or short Aeropress brews. Many work well in other methods too this is a curated selection, not an exhaustive list.

Siphon coffees

Siphon brewers maintain stable temperatures and use cloth filters to produce clean, intense cups that highlight bright notes. This collection offers coffees we’ve especially enjoyed in siphons, serving as suggestions rather than a complete list.
