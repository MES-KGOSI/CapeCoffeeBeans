About Cape Coffee Beans (CCB)

Cape Coffee Beans is a business dedicated to South African specialty coffee lovers. We sell everything from artisan-roasted beans to brewing equipment, espresso machines, home roasters, and everything in between. We serve passionate home consumers, as well as businesses that care about coffee. Our mission is to provide everything you need to make, serve and enjoy a delicious cup. On this page, you can learn a little bit more about who we are and our history.

How CCB Started

My name is Phaedon, and I founded Cape Coffee Beans in 2013 after working in technology-focused strategy consulting and South African eCommerce. My growing passion for coffee led me to ask, “why can’t I buy a good selection of freshly roasted beans online?” What began as a weekend project quickly grew into a side business and eventually my full-time venture. While the original idea was to sell beans only, I soon realised customers also needed grinders and brewing equipment. By expanding into all things specialty coffee, Cape Coffee Beans took off — and the rest is history.

What CCB is today

Today, CCB is a full-scale business, offering freshly roasted beans, a wide range of brewing gear, and both domestic and commercial espresso equipment. We deliver nationwide and internationally, and from our Claremont shop — CCBHQ — we serve the local community while dispatching to thousands of customers across South Africa and beyond.

The CCB Team

Behind CCB is a dedicated team of coffee enthusiasts who share a passion for specialty coffee and great customer service. From logistics to support, we focus on doing things well, owning our mistakes, and constantly improving. This commitment to quality and growth is what helps us deliver the experience our customers value.
